Oksusu Highball

Corn

CButtery, nutty, smokey and heavy, bringing out the 'American gambit'. The taste of overcooked corn, almost burnt in ghee, brings peace of mind.

Recipe

1.Pour 30 milliliters of homemade bourbon whiskey (mix dried corn overcooked in a pan with melted butter with 1 bottle of bourbon whiskey and infuse in the freezer for a day) into a long glass filled with ice.

2.Fill the glass with oolong tea and mix well.
