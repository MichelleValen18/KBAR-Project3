Maesil

Maesil-cha or plum tea is a traditional Korean tea made from maesil, omae, or maesil-cheong. Helps improve digestion, relieve constipation, and promote healthy bowel movements.

Recipe

1. Pour 40 milliliters of homemade plum wine (200 grams of plum extract, 10 shiso leaves, 1 bottle of gin, then filtered) into a long glass filled with ice.

2. Fill the remaining space with tonic water.
