Golden Harvest

Mugwort and Perilla leaves

Mugwort has been used in traditional systems of medicine in different parts of the world. The Perilla leaves are used fresh as ssam (wraps) at Korean barbecues. When prepared in a variety of other ways, they also make some great banchan.

Recipe

In a long glass filled with ice, mix seasonal herbs (seasonal herbs mixed with alcohol in a blender or tear by hand and soak in alcohol to maximize flavor; strain ingredients and leave in refrigerator for a day; use only the clear portion) and tonic water at a ratio of 1. Pour in :3.

2. Sprinkle a few drops of oil made by mixing water parsley, sesame oil, and salt.
