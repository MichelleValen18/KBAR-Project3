Recipe

1.Pour mushroom gin (sous vide the shiitake and pine mushrooms separately, distill under reduced pressure, and blend the two gins after distillation) into a long glass filled with ice.

2.Brew pine needle tea and add carbonation to make soda water. The ratio of host and soda water is 1:3.

3.Pickle the remaining mushrooms after distillation in soy sauce and dashi and serve as a garnish.
