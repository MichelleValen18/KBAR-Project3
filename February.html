Pomegranate

The pomegranate is a fruit-bearing deciduous shrub in the family Lythraceae, subfamily Punicoideae, that grows between 5 and 10 m tall.

Recipe

1.Put 2 tablespoons of pomegranate eggs in a highball glass, muddle lightly and fill with ice.

2.Slowly pour 30 milliliters of whiskey.

3. Fill your glass with your choice of club soda, tonic water, or ginger ale.
