Merry Tonic

Dill

The poinsettia is a commercially important flowering plant species of the diverse spurge family Euphorbiaceae. It is particularly well known for its red and green foliage and is widely used in Christmas floral displays.

Recipe

1.Add 45 milliliters of gin and 10 milliliters of lime juice to a highball glass filled with ice.

2. Fill the remaining space with tonic water.

3. Garnish with dill, pink, peppercorns, lemon, lime, and mint.
