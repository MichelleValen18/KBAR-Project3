Green Tea

Green tea is a type of tea that is made from Camellia sinensis leaves and buds that have not undergone the same withering and oxidation process which is used to make oolong teas and black teas.

Recipe

1. Pour 45 milliliters of whiskey into a long glass filled with ice.

2. Pour 130 milliliters of green tea soda. At home, you can use whiskey, chilled green tea, or lemon carbonated water.

3. Finish with lemon garnish.
