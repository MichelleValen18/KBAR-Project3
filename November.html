Kimchi Marg

Kimchi and Sweet Potato

Kimchi, is a traditional Korean banchan consisting of salted and fermented vegetables, most commonly using napa cabbage or Korean radish. Kimchi is also used in a variety of soups and stews.

Recipe

1. Milky wash in a long glass filled with ice (pour tequila, lime, agave syrup, and whole kimchi, then pour in milk and ferment for a day)

2. Milky washed kimchi margarita Fill the glass with 3 parts ginger ale.

3. Top with sweet potato foam.
