Blossom Bliss

Cherry Blossoms & Rape Flower

Cherry blossom trees are cultivated and grown for their beautiful flowers. As for Rape flowers it is a winter or spring annual oil crop in the Brassica family. It is also known as rape and oilseed rape.

Recipe

1. Pour 45 milliliters of gin into a round glass filled with ice.

2. Pour 130 milliliters of cherry blossom & rape tonic water. At home, steep cherry blossom and rape blossom tea bags in gin, then pour in tonic water.

3. It is prettier when flowers are added.
