Cheon Hye-hyang

A highball made with a Negroni cocktail made with nutty Gosori liquor and a carbonated drink added. Freshly squeezed Cheonhyehyang juice is floated and allowing you to feel the refreshing scent and bittersweet layer.

Recipe

1.Pour 20 milliliters each of Gosori liquor, Campari, and sweet vermouth into a long glass filled with ice.

2.Add 60 milliliters of tonic water.

3. Finish by gently adding 60 milliliters of Cheonhyehyang juice.
